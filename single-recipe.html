Boreg

With just four straightforward ingredients (and a fifth if you're feeling adventurous), creating the perfect Armenian Cheese Boreg is hassle-free. Using ready-made puff pastry, you're all set to whip up golden, crispy cheese-filled delights!

For many Armenian households, Boreg is a regular treat. I'm yet to come across an Armenian who hasn't relished a boreg in some variant or another. Each family boasts its unique take on this classic pastry. My grandma, for instance, used to craft her own dough and opted to fry them as opposed to baking.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Begin by letting your frozen puff pastry thaw overnight in your fridge. The next day, spread out the dough and slice it into square shapes. Alternatively, you can create your own homemade dough.

step 2

Stretch each part until it's about 10 inches long. Now, divide each stretched part into 3-4 sections width-wise and three lengthwise, leading to about 24 squares in total for both sheets.

step 2

As for the filling, it's a straightforward process. Just grate your Chicago cheese, and mix in some freshly chopped parsley. Add a generous spoonful of this mix onto each square.

step 3

Fold the squares over the cheese blend, shaping them into triangles or rectangles. To seal, simply press down with a fork along the edges. Place these cheese-filled delights on a parchment-covered baking tray, glaze with an egg wash, and send them to bake.
